Welcome to Maya

A sanctuary where
nature meets luxury.

Perched atop Sun Hill in Maya, Wakiso, our eco-resort is a world away from the city — yet only thirty minutes from it. Wander through preserved forest alive with birdsong and resident vervet monkeys, watch the famous fountain dance at dusk, and fall asleep to the quiet of the hills. Opened in 2018 as a forty-year dream, Maya is Uganda's hidden paradise, finally ready to be discovered.

“Where the sun rises and sets.”

Untouched Nature

12 hectares of preserved forest, wildlife, and hilltop views.

The Sun Hill

Golden sunrises and sunsets you won't find anywhere else.

World-Class Events

A 3,000-seat hall and gardens for up to 10 weddings at once.

Minutes Away

Just 22 km from Kampala on the Masaka highway.

The Signature Attraction

Africa's only
dancing fountain.

As the sun sets over Sun Hill, our legendary fountain comes alive — over 1.2 million colour variations rising and falling in a hypnotic display. Guests have mistaken it for Dubai. We simply call it home. It is the most photographed spot at Maya, and the reason so many evenings here end in wonder.
